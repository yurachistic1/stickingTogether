Sticking together for a good cause

Buy stickers, support the community!

What we do

Talented illustrators donate either signature or custom designs to support a cause

We turn these designs into high-quality vinyl stickers

Profits from the sale of stickers are donated to the stipulated charitable organisation

About

Sticking Together is a fundraiser started by two pals who just wanted to spread love and give back to the world through a shared obsession with stickers!

By purchasing our stickers, you’ll be supporting a fundraiser for a meaningful cause. The first cause we’re supporting is the support of victims of the Beirut explosion. Victims of this catastrophe, many of whom have lost their homes, are being sheltered and cared for by the Lebanese Red Cross. All the profits from the sale of stickers in the Lebanese Red Cross sticker collection will go to this organisation.

Check out the sticker collection or learn more about Sticking Together!
